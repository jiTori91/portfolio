maps

Located on planets across the Outlands‚ these massive landscapes host the massive Apex Games.

explore

Broken Moon Cleo, once whole, was shattered by a meteor that set Boreas on a path to ruin. Project Hope sought to restore the moon‚ turning it into a hub of research‚ homes‚ and industry as interest shifted. Now‚ Cleo hosts both innovation and the Apex Games, still striving to save Boreas.

E–District Once a thriving island of luxury‚ nightlife‚ and cuisine‚ Electro District was the heart of Suotamo. A storm knocked out its power‚ leading to riots and unrest. Legend Alter's ship crash released toxins‚ forcing a full evacuation. The Syndicate rebuilt the area for the Apex Games‚ restoring its neon glory. Now‚ E-District shines again—this time as a fierce arena for Legends.

Kingscanyon Kings Canyon grew from a Solace settlement into an IMC research hub‚ complete with Thunderdome‚ the arena that inspired the Apex Games. Beneath the island‚ the IMC secretly tested Phase tech and built a simulacra army. Though disasters reshaped the land‚ Syndicate projects keep it alive as the birthplace of the Games.

Olympus Olympus was a floating utopia for the Outlands' brightest minds until a Phase Rift accident drove everyone away. Hammond Robotics and the Syndicate turned it into an Apex Games arena. Disasters like Medusa Vines and failed relocations reshaped the city‚ creating new challenges for Legends.

Storm Point Storm Point was rich with resources, but violent tropical storms forced the nearby city of Suotamo to take over. After the IMC withdrew‚ failing storm catchers worsened the storms‚ leaving the island mostly abandoned. Now‚ Apex Games competitors face deadly wildlife and reactivated IMC defenses across the island.

Worlds Edge Talos was once uninhabitable until the IMC found rare minerals and cooled its lava flows‚ creating World's Edge. A tower explosion caused a deadly flash freeze‚ leaving the city abandoned except for Bloodhound's hunter tribe. The Syndicate and Hammond Robotics later returned‚ further destabilizing the land. Now‚ World's Edge serves as both a battleground for the Apex Games and a key hub for Hammond Robotics.
